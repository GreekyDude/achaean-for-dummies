Tips

Start with Sign → Syllable to build recognition. Once you know the vowels (a, e, i, o, u) and common consonant series (t, k, n, p, wa), move to Read a Word. The q-series (qa, qe, qo) and z-series (za, ze, zo) are easy to confuse with similar-looking signs — pay special attention to them.
