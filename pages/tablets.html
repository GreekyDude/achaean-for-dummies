How to read a Linear B tablet

Each line typically contains: a label or heading word (often a title or place name), followed by a commodity ideogram (a pictographic sign), followed by a number. The ideogram tells you what is being counted even if you can't read the words. Word dividers (short vertical strokes, transcribed as commas) separate entries.
