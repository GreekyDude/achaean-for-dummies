The Number System

Linear B uses a decimal additive system. Stack signs to build any number.

Numbers use Aegean numerals (Unicode U+10100+). The system is strictly additive: 𐄇=1, 𐄙=10, 𐄢=100, 𐄫=1000. Numbers always follow the commodity ideogram they quantify on the tablet.

How numbers appear on tablets

A typical line: 𐀷𐀙𐀏 𐄫𐄢𐄙𐄇 = wa-na-ka [1111 units of grain]. The ideogram between the label and the number tells you what is being counted even without reading the words.
